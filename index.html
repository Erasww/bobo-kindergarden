❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before enrolling your child.

What age do you accept children from?
+
We welcome children from 18 months to 6 years old across our various programs. Our Nursery program starts at 1.5 years, and our Pre-K and Kindergarten programs are available from age 2.5 to 6.
What languages are used in teaching?
+
We offer a trilingual curriculum in English, Kazakh, and Russian. Each day includes dedicated time in each language, giving children a strong foundation in all three. Language immersion begins naturally through songs, stories, and play.
How do you handle dietary requirements and allergies?
+
We take food safety very seriously. Parents provide detailed dietary information during enrollment, and our certified nutritionist creates custom meal plans. We are a nut-free facility and accommodate all allergies, intolerances, and cultural dietary requirements.
What is the teacher-to-child ratio?
+
We maintain a 1:6 ratio for Nursery (under 2 years), 1:8 for Pre-K, and 1:10 for Kindergarten. This ensures every child receives individual attention and care throughout their day.
How do you communicate with parents?
+
We use our dedicated parent app for daily updates, photos, and reports. Each child receives a weekly learning summary. We hold monthly parent-teacher meetings and host termly open days. Our door is always open!
Is there a trial day available?
+
Absolutely! We encourage all prospective families to book a free trial day. Your child spends a full session with us (accompanied by a parent if under 2), and you'll get a tour of our facilities and meet the team.
